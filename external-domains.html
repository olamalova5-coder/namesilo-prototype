Frequently Asked Questions
What does "External Domains" mean?
External Domains are domains registered at other registrars — like GoDaddy, Namecheap, or Google Domains. You can add them here to track expiration dates and renewal prices all in one place, without having to transfer them.
Does adding a domain here change anything at my current registrar?
No. Adding a domain to External Domains is read-only — it doesn't affect your domain, DNS settings, or anything at your current registrar. It simply lets you track and compare renewal costs from NameSilo.
How do I add my domains?
Click "Add External Domains", then paste your domain names (one per line or comma-separated). You can also upload a CSV file. We'll look up the registrar and expiration data automatically.
What is the "Renew with NameSilo" price?
This is the price you'd pay if you transferred and renewed this domain at NameSilo. NameSilo is one of the most affordable registrars — renewals include free WHOIS privacy, unlike most competitors.
Can I transfer a domain directly from here?
Yes. Once your domain is listed, click "Transfer to NameSilo" on any row. We'll walk you through the transfer process — it usually takes 5–7 days and automatically renews the domain for one year.
